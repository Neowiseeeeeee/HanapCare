Most Important Change

Right now your system is using:

Patients
Appointments
Consultations
Billing

for many roles.

I would convert them into role-focused labels:

Doctor:

My Patients
My Appointments
Prescriptions

Nurse:

Assigned Patients
Medication Administration

Receptionist:

Patient Registration
Scheduling

Cashier:

Financial Operations

This small change makes HanapCare feel like a real enterprise hospital system rather than a shared CRUD dashboard with different hidden menus.

Also, beyond hiding the sidebar items, implement route-level RBAC. A Cashier manually entering /patients should receive a 403 Access Denied page, not the patient list. This is a major professionalism upgrade and something reviewers often notice.
